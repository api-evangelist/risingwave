This article compares RisingWave and Apache Flink for Apache Iceberg ingestion, arguing that RisingWave delivers production-ready capabilities as native system features. Rather than treating Iceberg writes as a connector problem, RisingWave integrates exactly-once guarantees, commit scheduling, compaction, and query serving into a unified streaming database platform.
