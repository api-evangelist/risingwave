The article argues that Kafka compatibility may become less critical as AI agents reshape infrastructure architecture. With upstream consolidating around Postgres CDC, downstream standardizing on Apache Iceberg, and coding agents eliminating protocol learning costs, the next generation of message queues might prioritize simplicity and agent-friendliness over ecosystem compatibility.
